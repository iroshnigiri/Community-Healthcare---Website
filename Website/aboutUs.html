OUR MISSION

1. To provide safe, secure, and high quality medical care.

4. To promote advanced medical care and disseminate it to the world.



RESEARCH

1. To develop advanced healthcare technology and novel diagnostic and therapeutic methods and disseminate the techniques globally.

3. To enhance the functions of the Center for Clinical Research,foster a clinical research ethos, and promote clinical research and trials.
