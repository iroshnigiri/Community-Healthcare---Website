Standard Operating Procedure


  1. Covid test necessary for admission of patient

  2. Separate Rooms/Div., enry/exit points, and Staff for covid and non-covid patients

  3. Visitors can meet Covid patients virtually only

  4. 1Hr fixed time for visitors meeting patients physically

  5. Only 2 (max) visitors can visit patient daily with proper entries

  6. PPE kits necessary for staff as well as visitors

  7. Cleaning at 9PM and 6PM with phenolic disinfectants

  8. High Contact surfaces such as lift buttuons, doors, windows, toilets to be cleaned frequently

  9. Disinfect all cleaning equipment after use and before using in other area

  10. Discard cleaning material made of cloth in appropriate bags after cleaning and wear new pair of gloves.

  11. All disposable PPE should be discarded after cleaning activities are completed.
